# 🍕 Homemade Pizza Recipe

## Ingredients
- 2 cups flur
- 1 tsp yeast
- 1 cup warm water
- Tomato Sauce
- Mozzarella cheese
- Toppings of choice

- ## Instructions
- 1. Mix flour, yeast, and water to form dough.
- 2. Let rise dor 1 hour
- 3. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
- 4. Roll out dough and add sauce, cheese, and toppings.
- 5. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
